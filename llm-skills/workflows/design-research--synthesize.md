Synthesize qualitative research data into structured insights:

1. Use the the affinity-diagram skill (see skills/design-research--affinity-diagram.md) to organize raw data into themed clusters with insight statements
2. Use the the jobs-to-be-done skill (see skills/design-research--jobs-to-be-done.md) to map user motivations and outcome expectations
3. Combine findings into a prioritized list of design implications and opportunities
4. After completion, suggest: "Want me to the discover workflow to create personas from these insights?" or "Want me to the test-plan workflow to validate these findings with usability testing?"
